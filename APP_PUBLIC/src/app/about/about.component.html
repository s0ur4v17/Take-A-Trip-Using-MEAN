About Take A Trip

Take A Trip is a pioneer in Canada's online travel business, having grown from the root of a single brilliant idea: to empower the traveller. Take A Trip was created to provide the Canadian traveller with rapid bookings and a wide range of options. The company began its journey by addressing the US-Canada travel market by providing a variety of high-value products and services backed by technology and round-the-clock customer service.

Take A Trip has solidified its market position as a brand known for its dependability and openness.!
